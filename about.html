About Darb Tyres Trading

A UAE-based wholesale tyre supplier delivering genuine products and dependable service to dealers, workshops and fleets.

Our Story

A regional tyre trading partner

Established in Ajman in 2021, Darb Tyres Trading L.L.C supplies a complete range of car, 4×4, van, truck and bus tyres to the UAE and surrounding markets.

We focus on what matters to our customers: genuine products, reliable stock, competitive wholesale pricing and a direct line of support for every order.
